About

From Little League dreams to high school and college victories, my passion for baseball has shaped my life. Inspired by the game, I created this website to share my journey and showcase my growth through the years. You'll find stories from my time on the field, stats that highlight my performance, and insights from both high school and college ball.

In each phase of my baseball career, I've gained valuable experiences that pushed me to improve. Whether it was the thrill of a close game or the dedication required to refine my skills, baseball has been a constant in my life, driving me to achieve more. This site is a tribute to those moments and the inspiration they provided.

  • Showcases my career in baseball through little league, highschool, and college
  • Memorable games that defined my passion and competitive spirit.
  • Key high school and college stats that showcase my growth and dedication to the sport.
  • Personal reflections on the lessons learned through wins, losses, and perseverance.
